Privacy Policy — Monster Reincarnation

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Monster Reincarnation is a free browser game. We respect your privacy and collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what happens to data when you play.

1. Data the game stores

Your game progress (your save) is stored locally in your own browser (via localStorage) on your device. We do not require an account, and we do not collect, sell, or store your personal information on our servers. You can erase your save at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Anonymous, aggregated gameplay data (such as which features are used) may be used to balance and improve the game. This data contains no information that personally identifies you.

2. Advertising (Google AdSense)

The supported version of the game may display ads served by Google AdSense. In connection with this:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices.
  • More on how Google uses data from sites that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

The ad-free version of the game (hosted on GitHub Pages) shows no ads and loads no advertising code.

3. Cookies

The game itself uses local storage (not cookies) to keep your save. Advertising cookies, where present, are set by Google and its partners as described above.

4. Visitors from the EEA, UK and Switzerland

Where required, ads are served subject to a consent mechanism, and you may choose whether to allow personalised ads.

5. Children

This game is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above always reflects the current version.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact: armyhell07@gmail.com
